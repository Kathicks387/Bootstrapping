About Me

After a succesful career in the field of education, I have decided to pursue a career in computer programing. After seeing my first through fifth grade students create programs through the Tynker Program, I knew it was time for me to fulfill my desire to become a computer programmer. With one hundred percent certainty that this was the path I wanted to take, I enrolled in the Coding Bootcamp Program offered by Case Western Reserve University. The program is very challenging, but I'm learning a ton!

Thus far I have gained knowledge, practice and experience with using HTML, CSS, Javascript, Handlebars, Node, Express,PWA, React, MySQL and Mongodb programming languages. I have highlighted a few of my creations on my Profile page and you will find my group projects on my Group Projects page. My github repository can be found at https://github.com/Kathicks387. I look forward to learning more as I venture into the final two months of the program. Upon completion of the program I will eagerly await the opportunity to apply all I have learned.
